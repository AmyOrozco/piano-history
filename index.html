About Me

Hi, I am Amy Orozco. I have played paino since I was ten. I took piano classes for four years because I didn't know anything about piano. After four years I got busy in school and had other priorities and decided to stop going to classes. I thought it would be better to just continue to practice and learn by myself at home since I had already learned a lot over four years. I still practice a little bit but I have not gone to classes ever since.
